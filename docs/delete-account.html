Delete Your Account

We're sorry to see you go. Here's how to delete your CalorieLens account and associated data.

How to request deletion

Send an email to the address below from the email associated with your CalorieLens account. We will process your request within 7 business days.

Request Account Deletion

Or email makhuvhap.c@gmail.com with the subject line "CalorieLens Account Deletion Request".

Steps we take

1
We verify your identity using the email address on your account.
2
Your account and all associated data are permanently deleted from our servers.
3
We send you a confirmation email once deletion is complete.

What data is deleted

Account profile Permanently deleted
Email address Permanently deleted
Meal diary entries Permanently deleted
Usage history Permanently deleted
Nutrition profiles Permanently deleted
Note: If you have an active Pro subscription, please cancel it through the Google Play Store before requesting account deletion. We cannot process refunds for active subscriptions — this is managed by Google Play.

Data stored on your device

Data stored locally on your device (via the app) is not affected by account deletion. To remove local data, simply uninstall the CalorieLens app from your device.
