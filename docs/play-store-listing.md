# CalorieLens — Google Play Store Listing

## App Name (30 chars max)
CalorieLens - AI Nutrition

## Short Description (80 chars max)
Snap a photo of any meal and get instant AI-powered calorie and macro estimates.

## Full Description (4000 chars max)

CalorieLens uses advanced AI to make nutrition tracking effortless. Simply take a photo of your meal, and our AI instantly identifies each food item and provides detailed calorie and macronutrient estimates — no manual searching or logging required.

Whether you're managing your weight, building muscle, or simply eating more mindfully, CalorieLens gives you the tools to understand exactly what's on your plate.

HOW IT WORKS

Point your camera at any meal and tap scan. Within seconds, CalorieLens breaks down each food item with estimated calories, protein, carbs, and fat. You can also type a description of your meal or scan a product barcode for packaged foods.

Your scans are saved to a daily diary so you can track your intake throughout the day and see how each meal contributes to your overall goal.

KEY FEATURES

AI Meal Scanning — Photograph any meal for instant nutritional analysis. The AI recognises individual food items, estimates portion sizes, and calculates calories and macros.

Barcode Scanner — Scan packaged food barcodes to pull nutrition data from a global database of over 3 million products, with AI fallback for items not found.

AI Nutrition Coach — Chat with a knowledgeable AI coach about your diet, get advice on healthier alternatives, and ask questions about nutrition.

Personalised Meal Plans — Browse curated meal plans designed for South African tastes and budgets, or let AI generate a custom plan based on your calorie goal and dietary preferences.

Fridge Scanner — Snap a photo of what's in your fridge and get AI-suggested recipes using the ingredients you already have.

Daily Diary & Tracking — Every meal is logged with a timestamp and full macro breakdown. Track your water intake and monitor your progress toward daily calorie goals.

Smart Calorie Goals — Set personalised daily targets based on your age, weight, height, and activity level. The app calculates your recommended intake and tracks your progress.

FREE & PRO TIERS

Free users get 5 AI meal scans per day with full access to the diary, barcode scanner, and curated meal plans. Upgrade to Pro for up to 50 scans per day, AI-generated personalised meal plans, Fridge Scanner, unlimited AI Coach access, and an ad-free experience.

PRIVACY FIRST

Your data is stored securely with row-level security — only you can access your information. Meal photos are analysed in real time and never stored on our servers. Guest users can try the app without creating an account, with all data kept locally on device.
